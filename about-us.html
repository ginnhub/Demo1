About Us

We are a small business based in Australia that specializes in phone and tablet cases for popular brands such as Apple and Samsung. Our business is setup for online sales only to keep the costs down and pass on all of the savings to you. By offering our products online we can save you up to 80% of retail prices. All of our products are great quality and come with a 60 money back or exchange guarantee. Customer service is the most important aspect of our business. We like to offer personal and genuine customer service rather than the robotic style you get from large corporations.

Our Aim



Competitive Prices



We source products directly from the manufacturer and keep our margins as low as possible to ensure you get a great price.

Quality Products



We only source good products that have been tested and reviewed before releasing to the public.

Fast Shipping



All of our products are posted within 1 to 2 business days of receiving cleared payment and all products are posted from Australia NOT overseas.

Great Communication.



We respond to customer questions as fast as possible. All emails are responded to within 24 hours and more often than not much sooner.
